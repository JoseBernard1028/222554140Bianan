Tribute to GLOCO

A journey through the life of a beloved streamer and vlogger

Who is GLOCO?

GLOCO, whose real name is Gian Lois Concepcion, is a Filipino content creator known for his engaging streams, entertaining vlogs, and captivating Let's Plays. His unique personality and humor have garnered a loyal following in the gaming community.

Career Highlights

GLOCO's rise to fame began with his hilarious voice-over dubs and gameplay videos. His content spans various genres, from horror games to quirky indie titles, making him a versatile and beloved figure in the gaming scene.

  • Known for his Pinoy Horror Let's Plays
  • Collaborations with other prominent Filipino YouTubers
  • Recognition in gaming and vlogging awards

Influence and Impact

GLOCO's content has not only entertained but also inspired many aspiring streamers and vloggers. His down-to-earth approach and dedication to his craft have made him a role model for fans and content creators alike.
